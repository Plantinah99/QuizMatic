About Math Quiz App

Math Quiz App was born out of a passion for making mathematics accessible and enjoyable for everyone. As someone who once struggled with math, I wanted to create a tool that would have helped me in my learning journey. This app aims to make math practice more engaging, effective, and fun for students of all ages.
